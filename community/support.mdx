## How to use Inference.net Support

Inference.net Support is meant to cover as much ground as we can with common issues faced by the community during the Inference.net user journey. If it's your first time, here are the steps you should take to maximize Inference.net Support.

1. Make sure you meet all the [system requirements](/hardware) for Inference.net, including your GPU, RAM, and disk space.
2. Make sure your WiFi is reliable and has at least 100Mbps of bandwidth. You can test it here: [Fast.com](https://fast.com/)
3. Make sure you have the latest version of Inference.net [downloaded](https://testnet.inference.net/download) and installed.

You may opt to [Submit an issue](https://github.com/context-labs/kuzco-docs/issues/new?title=Feedback%20for%20"FAQs"&labels=feedback) or [open a support ticket](https://discord.gg/kuzco).
